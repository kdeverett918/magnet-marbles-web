Magnet Marbles — Android

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Summary

The Magnet Marbles Android app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. It runs fully offline. Everything it stores stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing is collected or sent off your device. The app has no user accounts, no login, no servers, and makes no network requests. The Android build ships with the INTERNET permission removed. There is no advertising SDK and no third-party analytics SDK in the app.

What is stored on your device

The game saves the following locally on your device only (it is never uploaded):

  • Game progress: coins, battle-pass XP and tier, daily-challenge state, best scores.
  • Your equipped cosmetic marble skin and unlocked cosmetics.
  • Settings: audio, haptics, and control preferences.

Analytics

The app contains a lightweight diagnostics logger used during development that writes only to a local, on-device log. It transmits nothing — no analytics data leaves your device.

Advertising and purchases

The current build contains no advertising and no in-app purchases. If this changes in a future version, this policy and the Google Play Data Safety section will be updated.

Data sharing, retention, and deletion

We do not share any data with third parties, because we do not collect any. Because no data is sent to us, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on a server. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored game data from your device; you may also clear the app's storage from your device settings at any time.

Permissions

The app requests no sensitive runtime permissions (no location, contacts, camera, microphone, or storage access beyond its own sandboxed app data).

Children's privacy

The game is suitable for general audiences and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes and contact
